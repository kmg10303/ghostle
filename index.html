How to play.

After you play a letter, the computer will automatically place it's own, random, letter which correlates with a word in its database. Try to gain as much information as you can about the secret word before the final row!

If you are super confident, click the "GUESS" button to bypass the computer, but be warned... if you guess wrong, you lose, no matter what row you are on!

The guess button is automatically selected on the last row of the puzzle.

Enjoy!
